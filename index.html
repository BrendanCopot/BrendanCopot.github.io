Sky Surge

Sky Surge is a Speedrun-Platformer where the player explores the fantasy ruins of the Skyreach Archipelago! Find forgotten artifacts and soar at breakneck speeds by utilizing various abilities and the environment to maintain your momentum! Large beasts not seen by man for millennia will cross your path. It is up to your own sense of adventure to see where they take you...
